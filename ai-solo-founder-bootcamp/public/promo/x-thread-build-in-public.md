**Tweet 4/12**

Dimension 1 · Cash runway

Q: How many months of living expenses do you have saved?

0-3 months: 0 pts
3-6 months: 5 pts
6-12 months: 14 pts (recommended floor)
12+ months / passive income: 20 pts

OPC isn't poor-founder entrepreneurship. If you can't survive to PMF, you fail.

**Tweet 7/12**
