JR Academy × CloudTech Melbourne 03 / 12 — The Subject
The subject of the workshop · Direct from openclaw.ai

"The AI that actually does things."

OpenClaw is an open-source personal AI assistant by Austrian developer Peter Steinberger (@steipete, creator of PSPDFKit). You talk to it via WhatsApp / Telegram / Discord / Slack — it clears your inbox, sends emails, manages your calendar, even checks you in for flights. Everything runs on your own machine. Recently partnered with VirusTotal for Skill Security.
What makes it new

Actually executes — not just chats.

ChatGPT tells you how to unsubscribe. OpenClaw does it for you. That's the category jump.

Where it lives

On your computer, via your phone.

Installed locally. Your data stays on your device. You control it from WhatsApp / Telegram wherever you are.

Self-extending

Writes its own Skills.

Say "connect to Todoist" — it authors the plugin, tests it, and uses it next time. No code, no API docs.

JR Academy × CloudTech Melbourne 04 / 12 — The Problem
The problem we solve

"I've watched the AI demos,
but nothing is running on my laptop."

Every knowledge worker has watched hundreds of AI demos, followed dozens of influencers, bookmarked "must-have" AI tool lists. Yet on their actual laptop, only ChatGPT is open. The gap between awareness and adoption is massive — and it's where most paid AI courses fail. This 2.5-hour evening workshop closes that gap in one sitting.

Typical online AI course

  • Video lectures on concepts
  • No real installation, no real account setup
  • 70% of buyers never finish
  • Attendees leave with notes, not tools
  • "I'll set it up this weekend" — they never do

Our OpenClaw workshop

  • Installed on the attendee's own laptop — in the room
  • AI-guided "Quest" mode + 1:5-1:8 human tutor ratio
  • One real task completed before they leave
  • Personalized configuration that writes "like you"
  • Walks out with a working AI assistant, not a PDF
