## 二、关键写作规则

### ✅ DO

| 规则 | 为什么 |
|------|--------|
| **1 页搞定**（Big 4 / Banks）/ 1.5 页 OK（Tech / Gov / Engineering） | HR 7 秒判断 |
| 每个 bullet 用 **STAR + 量化 Result** | 没数字 = 没说服力 |
| 强动词开头：Led / Designed / Implemented / Analysed / Optimised / Negotiated | 体现主动性 |
| 关键词从目标 JD 抓 **top 20 名词 + 动词** | ATS 关键词匹配 > 75% |
| **Australian English** 拼写（Optimised / Organised / Centre） | 美式拼写会被识别为复制美国模板 |
| **Suburb + City** 标清楚（Carlton, VIC） | HR 看通勤距离 |
| Visa status 明确写（485 / PR / Citizen） | 留学生最大筛选维度 |
| LinkedIn URL **customised**（linkedin.com/in/your-name 不是 /your-name-abc123） | 一键看你完整背景 |

### ❌ DON'T

- 不写 "References available upon request"（废话）
- 不放头像（澳洲法律隐性反对，没人放）
- 不写出生日期 / 婚姻状况 / 性别（反歧视法）
- 不混用过去式 + 现在式（现职用现在式 / 过往用过去式）
- 不写 hobby（除非和岗位强相关，例如 Atlassian 招开源贡献者写 GitHub repo OK）
- 不用 helped / participated / assisted / was responsible for（弱动词）

## 三、STAR Bullet 改写 5 个对比

### Bullet 1 · Teamwork
- ❌ Helped team to organise events for international students
- ✅ **Led** 4-person committee organising **6 networking events for 200+ international students**; secured **$5k corporate sponsorship** from 3 partners

### Bullet 2 · Analytical / Data
- ❌ Responsible for cleaning customer data using Excel
- ✅ **Automated cleaning of 200+ weekly customer records** in Excel using nested formulas (VLOOKUP / INDEX-MATCH); **saved 6h/week** reporting time and **reduced data entry errors by 35%**

### Bullet 3 · Leadership
- ❌ Was a tutor for first-year students
- ✅ **Mentored 12 first-year students** in Accounting 101 through peer-tutoring program; **10/12 mentees achieved HD/Distinction** (vs cohort average 6/12)

### Bullet 4 · Customer focus
- ❌ Helped customers with their orders in cafe
- ✅ Served **80+ customers daily** during peak Sydney CBD lunch rush; achieved **94% upsell rate on combo deals**, contributing to **$12k/month additional revenue** for the store

### Bullet 5 · Initiative
- ❌ Worked on a project to improve marketing
- ✅ **Initiated** Instagram content calendar for 800-member student society; grew followers from **1.2k → 3.8k in 6 months** (+217%); 3 posts featured by university official account

## 四、ATS 关键词检查清单

简历改完，去 **[Jobscan](https://jobscan.co) 免费版**扫一遍：

1. 上传简历 PDF + 目标 JD 全文
2. 看 Match Rate — 目标 **> 75%**
3. 检查 missing keywords — 把目标 JD 里出现 ≥ 2 次的关键词补到简历里（Profile / Skills / Bullet 三处都行，但不要硬塞）
4. 重新扫一次，达标再投

**澳洲 GP 高频 ATS 关键词样例**（按行业）：

| 行业 | 高频关键词 |
|------|-----------|
| Big 4 Audit | financial statements, IFRS, audit procedures, risk assessment, client engagement |
| Banks | risk analysis, credit, compliance, AML/CTF, customer engagement, KYC |
| Gov Grad | stakeholder engagement, policy analysis, briefing, project management |
| Consulting | problem solving, structured thinking, client management, market research |
| Tech | agile, scrum, stakeholder communication, technical proficiency, cross-functional |
| Engineering | project lifecycle, safety, design specifications, sustainability, AS/NZS standards |
