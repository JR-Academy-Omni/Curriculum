## 出图 tips

- **45°/90° 严格**：显式 `routes bent only at 45 or 90 degrees, NOT curved`，否则模型画自由曲线
- **transfer station**：donut 形状，一定要写 `donut-shaped open circle for transfer stations, filled circle for terminals`
- **不要画地图轮廓**：明确 `abstract diagrammatic style, NOT geographic accuracy, no AU coastline`
- **station labels**：`monospace tiny labels next to each station dot`
- **4 条线颜色**：在 [TEXT] 显式写 4 条线 + 颜色 + Part 编号映射，否则模型会乱涂
