# PM Manager × AI FOMO × Instagram / LinkedIn Post (EN)

## What this poster says

The English version of the org-FOMO angle. LinkedIn manager crowd has moved past "I should learn AI" — their teams are already using AI. The next gap: "my company has 200 people using ChatGPT, and nobody owns the maturity assessment, the tool stack, the pilot plan, or the ROI dashboard." This bootcamp is the 8-week framework to turn scattered usage into an organizational adoption plan: maturity scoring, tool selection, pilot design, ADKAR change management, governance, ROI metrics, capstone deliverable.
