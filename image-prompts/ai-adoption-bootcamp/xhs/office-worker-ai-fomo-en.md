# Office Worker × AI FOMO × Instagram / LinkedIn Post (EN)

## What this poster says

Different from ai-essentials office-worker-ai-fomo (which is "your colleagues use ChatGPT, you don't") — this poster is for the office worker who has already started using AI and now sees the next opportunity: become the AI Champion their company doesn't have yet. Learn the org-level toolkit (maturity assessment, pilot design, change management, training, governance, ROI) that separates "I use AI" from "I help my company adopt AI." That's the promotion path. The bootcamp is 8 weeks · 22 live sessions · 39 interactive labs · capstone deliverable.
