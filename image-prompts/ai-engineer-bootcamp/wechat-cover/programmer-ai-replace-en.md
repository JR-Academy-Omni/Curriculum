## Generation tips

- **Check the apostrophe in "won't"**: image-2 occasionally renders smart quotes or curly quotes — explicitly say `straight ASCII apostrophe` if it glitches
- **If the two-color headline blends visually**: increase contrast — append `the second line in red #FF5757 should clearly stand out from the first line in off-white`
- **If hands look wrong** (extra fingers / distorted): replace visual with `a black mechanical keyboard with subtle backlight, no hands visible, focused on the keys`
- **If saturation is low**: append `the red #FF5757 must pop vividly against the dark navy background`
- **No need for Thinking mode** — only 2 text blocks, Instant handles this 95%+ on first try
