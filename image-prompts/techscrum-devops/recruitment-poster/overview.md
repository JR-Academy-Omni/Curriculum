## 出图 tips

- **白线一定要细**：显式 `1px white technical drawing strokes, NOT thick glowing lines`
- **dimension lines**：写 `dimension lines with small arrowheads at both ends, mono labels`
- **不要画 cloud 的 marketing 图**：明确 `schematic abstraction only, no marketing-style cloud graphics`
- **REV.0N 标签**：在 [TEXT] 显式标注每张卡的 REV 号
- **黄色仅在 callouts**：不要让 yellow 散到 background，蓝图里黄色就是"关键标注"
